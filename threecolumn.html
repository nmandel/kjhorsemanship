About me

I have been passionate about horses from a very young age. I sat on my first horse at around 2, spent my childhood taking lessons, riding and caring for my horse, and spending every second of free time at the barn all the way up until I took my horse with me to college. I graduated from Feather River College with degrees in Equine Training, Pack Skills, and Ranch Skills. Every day I work to further my knowledge and skill in training horses. I have experience in a wide range of breeds and disciples.
