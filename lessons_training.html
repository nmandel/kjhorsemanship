Lessons and Training

I specialize in, but am not limited to, starting horses, building understanding, respect, and communication with troubled horses, tune ups, Western, English, and Liberty.


Training

Training is 5 days a week, about an hour session. Part of training includes the option of one instructional day a week. Ninety days is the minimum for starting a horse.

  • Onsite training at South Bay Horse Ranch (located in Gilroy / San Martin): $500/month. Owner is responsible for paying board.
  • Training at your facility: $650 and up, depending on location.


Lessons

  • All ages and experience levels welcome.
  • Tell me your goals and we can progress toward making them reality.
  • I can provide a lesson horse (for those under 160 pounds) or I can teach you on your personal horse.
    For one hour lessons:
    • Onsite at South Bay Horse Ranch: $50.
    • At your facility: $60 and up depending on your location
    For one and half hour lessons:
    • Onsite at South Bay Horse Ranch: $65.
    • At your facility: $75 and up depending on your location
  • Scheduled lessons must be paid at the beginning of the month, or at the time of your first lesson, whichever comes first. You are responsible for being on time.
  • Riders under 18 must wear a helmet.
