Katie came along and I knew that she was the right one. The way she interacted with her own horse, Colonel, showed me that she is not only kind to people, but also to horses. I still have so much to learn, and Katie is the one to teach me. She loves children, young adults, and everyone loves her back. I cannot recommend Katie enough.   • "When Katie gives direction she does it with respect and makes sure that I understand what I am asking my horse and why. She is patient with me when I am nervous or slow to 'get it' and she doesn’t get mad at me or my horse. This helps my horse and me gain confidence and trust to move on to the next step.
    She is horse and people savvy, competent, easygoing, friendly, truly approachable and there are no stupid questions. She will correct you without making you feel inept and will explain what you are telling your horse from his viewpoint. You might think that you are clearly telling him to do something but he keeps doing the 'wrong' thing. Sometimes it turns out that he is doing exactly what you are telling him to do.
